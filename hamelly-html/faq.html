How Hamelly Works

1

Request

Tap each delivery option to see wait time, sizes and price. Chose a flat fee of $30 Pick up Truck, $40 Flat Bed Truck, $50 Box Truck in your county. Then enter your pickup location and tap request—your driver will arrive in minutes.

2

Deliverys

Your driver comes to you. You’ll see your driver’s contact information and vehicle details in the app, so you know you’re getting in the right car.

3

Pay and go

Hop out and rate your driver when you reach your destination. We automatically charge the credit card on file, so you never need cash.
